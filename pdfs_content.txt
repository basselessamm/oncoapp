OncoRepurpose AI 
A Clinical Decision Support & Research App for Drug Repurposing in Oncology 
 
🧠 App Overview 
OncoRepurpose AI is a mobile application that helps clinicians, researchers, and students 
identify repurposed drugs for cancer treatment by integrating: 
• 
Drug databases 
• 
Molecular pathways 
• 
AI-based recommendations 
• 
Molecular docking (binding affinity) 
👉 The goal: faster, evidence-based, and mechanistically supported cancer therapy insights 
 
🎯 Core Objectives 
• 
Discover new uses for existing drugs 
• 
Provide mechanism-based explanations 
• 
Support personalized oncology decisions 
• 
Integrate bioinformatics + structural biology 
 
⚙️ FULL APP FEATURES (Integrated) 
 
🔍 1. Cancer-Based Search Engine 
Users enter: 
• 
Cancer type (e.g., breast, lung) 
📊 Output: 
• 
List of repurposed drugs: 
o Propranolol 
o Metformin 
 
💊 2. Drug Intelligence Database 
Each drug includes: 
📌 Basic Info: 
• 
Original use 
• 
Repurposed oncology role 
🧬 Mechanism of Action: 
• 
Example: 
o Propranolol → inhibits β-adrenergic signaling 
o Metformin → activates AMPK → inhibits mTOR 
 
🧬 3. Pathway & Gene Analysis 
Displays: 
🎯 Target Genes: 
• 
VEGF → angiogenesis 
• 
BAX / BCL2 → apoptosis 
🔄 Pathways: 
• 
Angiogenesis 
• 
Apoptosis 
• 
mTOR signaling 
👉 Helps explain why the drug works 
 
📊 4. Evidence Level Indicator 
Each drug is labeled: 
• 
🟢 Clinical trials 
• 
🟡 In vitro / in vivo 
• 
🔵 Computational 
👉 Adds scientific credibility 
 
🤖 5. AI Recommendation System 
🧾 Input: 
• 
Gene expression profile 
(e.g., VEGF ↑, BAX ↓) 
⚡ Output: 
• 
Suggested drugs: 
o Propranolol 
🧠 Logic (simple rule-based): 
• 
VEGF ↑ → anti-angiogenic drugs 
• 
mTOR ↑ → Metformin 
 
🔗 6. Molecular Docking & Binding Affinity Module (KEY 
FEATURE) 
🎯 Purpose: 
Validate drug–protein interaction 
 
🧪 Example Display: 
Drug: Propranolol 
Target Protein: VEGFR 
Binding Affinity (ΔG): 
👉 -7.5 kcal/mol 
 
📊 Interpretation: 
• 
🟢 Strong binding (≤ -7) 
• 
🟡 Moderate (-5 to -7) 
• 
🔴 Weak (≥ -5) 
 
🔬 Interaction Details: 
• 
Hydrogen bonds 
• 
Hydrophobic interactions 
 
💡 What this adds: 
👉 Structural validation of drug action 
👉 Makes your app research-level
